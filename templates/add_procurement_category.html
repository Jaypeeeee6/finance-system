Category Guidelines

Naming Conventions:
  • Use clear, descriptive category names
  • Keep names consistent across departments
  • Use proper capitalization
  • Examples: Kitchen Tool, Dining, Stationary, Electrical, Furniture
Department:
  • Each category is specific to a department
  • Different departments can have different categories
  • Users will only see categories for their department
  • Choose the most appropriate department
