Location Priority Guidelines

Priority System:
  • Lower priority numbers appear first in branch dropdowns
  • Priority 1 appears first, Priority 2 appears second, etc.
  • You can reorder locations later by dragging them in the management page
  • Locations without a priority entry will appear last
Location Names:
  • Use clear, consistent names
  • Location names should match the "restaurant" field in branches
  • Examples: Office, Kucu, Boom, Thoum, Kitchen
Tip: After adding a location, you can assign branches to it when creating or editing branches.
